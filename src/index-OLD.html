Welcome

Part of the Pack is experienced pet care for Lower Cape Cod, MA. The Pack is fully insured and a member of Pet Sitters Associates. We will work with you to provide the care your pet needs! We offer dog walking, short-term pet-sitting, check-in visits, small animal care, and we're open to new ideas to fit the needs of our customers. Our goal is to get to know you and your animals well. We want to form working relationships with you, and we want to make your pets a part of our pack!

About the pack

Rachel & Riley
Riley

Part of the Pack was started by Rachel Whiting, a local graduate of Nauset High School and a student of Cape Cod Community College. Growing up her goal was always to work with animals. She began Part of the Pack to build up her experience with animals and with running a small business. And of course because she loves dogs!

The leader of the Pack and Rachel's best friend, Riley was the inspiration for 'Part of the Pack'. Riley was Rachel's first dog and even better business partner. He helped train many dogs, and kept the pack close while on walks.

In December of 2011 Riley passed away after battling with hemangiosarcoma (cancer of the blood vessels). There will always room in the car, and a ball in Rachel's purse for Riley. The time Rachel shared with Riley encourages her to make Part of the Pack a positive experience for pets and owners.
